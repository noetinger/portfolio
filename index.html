About Me

How much does a Polar Bear weigh? Enough to break the ice...

Nick Oetinger, a native to Buffalo, NY, a graduate of SUNY Oswego, and transplant to Charlotte, NC in 2018. I am a lover of running, rock climbing, hiking, and anything adventurous.

I spent 3 years teaching Technology and coaching Track at a public high school in Binghamton, NY. Following my teaching career, I spent a year working with 3D Printers and Additive Manufacturing Solutions at Duncan-Parnell in Charlotte, NC. Now, after completing a 6 month Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp at UNC Charlotte, I am ready to begin my journey as a Web Developer.

I consider myself an experienced and accomplished technologist with a plethora of technical skills, coupled with a problem solving mindset, and am seeking to grow into a developer role. I will always welcome any opportunity to learn and grow both personally and professionally.
